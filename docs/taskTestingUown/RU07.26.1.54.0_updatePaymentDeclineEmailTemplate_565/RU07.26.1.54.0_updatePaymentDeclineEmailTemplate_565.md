## Description

### Summary

Replace the legacy Payment Decline email templates with the new HubSpot-style design for both **Uown** and **Kornerstone (KS)**. The email is sent when a customer payment fails, triggered by `PaymentReceiptService` using template name `PaymentDeclineEmail` (KS accounts resolve to `KORNERSTONE_PaymentDeclineEmail` via `CorrespondenceService`). Follows the same approach used for the recent Application Decline email redesign (`decline-email.html` / `kornerstone/decline-email.html`).

### Background

Current templates are legacy HTML layouts:
- `src/main/resources/correspondence/templates/payment-decline-email.html`
- `src/main/resources/correspondence/templates/kornerstone/payment-decline-email.html`

Marketing provided a new HubSpot template with updated branding/layout/copy (Uown version attached to the ticket).

### Email Details (from provided template)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Subject | Action Needed: Issue Processing Your Recent Payment |
| Preview text | Ensure your account stays active by resolving payment issues. |
| Greeting | `[FIRST NAME], we had trouble processing your payment.` |
| CTA | Make a payment at `portal.uownleasing.com` + customer portal parameters |
| Support phone (Uown) | (877) 357-5474 |
| Closing | Thank you for choosing Uown. |

### Scope

1. **Uown template** — replace `payment-decline-email.html` with new HubSpot HTML; wire Thymeleaf placeholders: `[FIRST NAME]` → `${CommonDataPojo.customerFirstName}` (fallback `"Customer"` if empty, same as `decline-email.html`); portal link → `th:href="'https://portal.uownleasing.com' + ${CommonDataPojo.customerPortalParameters}"`; keep Uown branding (logo, social links, contact image, footer address/phone).
2. **Kornerstone template** — create KS variant under `kornerstone/payment-decline-email.html`, mirroring `kornerstone/decline-email.html` and `kornerstone/customer-portal-reminder-email.html`: KS logo (`kc-login-logo.png` or equivalent); portal URL via `${CommonDataPojo.customerPortalUrl}` + `${CommonDataPojo.customerPortalParameters}`; support phone **(888) 521-5111**; KS contact image, social links (Facebook/LinkedIn/Instagram), footer; closing "Thank you for choosing Kornerstone" (or equivalent).
3. **Template metadata** — update subject line in `correspondence/TemplateInfo.xlsx` (Uown) and `correspondence/templates/kornerstone/KS_Templates.xlsx` (Kornerstone); reload templates into DB after deploy via `TemplateManagementService`.
4. **Out of scope (unless required)** — no changes expected to `PaymentReceiptService` trigger logic; SMS template (`payment-decline-sms.txt`) unchanged unless requested separately.

**AC9 (enhancement, added via comment — Sowjanya Kaligineedi, 2026-06-19, NOT in the original description, must be covered):** conditional language for **settlement-arrangement** payment declines. A payment is a "settlement payment" when a payment arrangement exists for the ACH/CC payment AND `arrangement.getArrangementType()` equals `ArrangementType.SETTLEMENT`. In that case:
  - Replace "Unfortunately, we were unable to process your most recent payment." → "Unfortunately, we were unable to process your settlement payment."
  - Replace "To keep your account in good standing, please make a payment as soon as possible." → "To keep your settlement offer in good standing, please make a payment as soon as possible."
  - All other messaging unchanged.
  - Regression corollary: a decline WITHOUT a SETTLEMENT arrangement must keep the original (non-settlement) copy.
