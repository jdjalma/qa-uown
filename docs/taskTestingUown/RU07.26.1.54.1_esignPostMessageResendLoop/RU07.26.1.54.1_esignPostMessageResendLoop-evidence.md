# QA Validation - #1341 Origination | Retry PostMessage While in Control

**Environment:** sandbox + stg (build 2026-07-01, MR !1498 in R1.53.2)
**Result:** ⚠️ **Approved with follow-up - the post-sign relay works across both partners and both signing providers; 2 brief scenarios are code-confirmed pending a runtime path.**

---

> ### TL;DR
> The post-sign handoff resends `uown_success` every ~1s (safety ceiling 10s) then redirects, confirmed live on PayTomorrow and TireAgent, after both GowSign and SignWell signing, plus the non-postMessage 2s path. Deploy confirmed on sandbox and stg.
> Non-blocking findings: in the real embedded flow the partner advances and tears down the iframe within ~0.5 to 2.5s, so the loop almost never reaches 10 attempts (observed 1 to 6); the "Thrice"/10 count is a nominal ceiling.
> **Action:** Approve deploy. Two brief scenarios (cancel-before-signing, the complete-e-sign page) remain code-confirmed pending a bank-verification runtime path; track as follow-up.

---

## Summary

After a customer finishes signing, Origination now notifies the partner checkout that signing succeeded by posting `uown_success` to the parent window repeatedly (every second, up to ten seconds) and then redirects. This replaces the previous single-shot message, which could be missed by the partner page and leave the checkout stalled after the customer had already signed.

The behavior was validated by driving real signing ceremonies inside the actual partner portals (PayTomorrow embedded, PayPair for TireAgent) and by the standalone contract flow. The parent page reliably receives the message and advances its checkout exactly once, even though it receives several copies. Merchants configured without the relay redirect after two seconds with no message, as designed. No legacy behavior remains.

## Validated scenarios

### Task requirements

#### 1. Relay merchant resends the message then redirects

> ✅ **PASSED** · PayTomorrow (MSA Powersports `OL90402-0001`) TX and CO, TireAgent (`OW90218-0001`) OH

After signing, the success toast "Thank you for signing your lease. You will now be redirected..." and the loading overlay appear, the parent receives `uown_success` about once per second, and the page redirects. The relay fires identically after GowSign and after SignWell, proving it is independent of the signing provider.

**Evidence:** merchantCode `OL90402-0001` / `OW90218-0001`; iframe `secure-sandbox.uownleasing.com/{token}/complete`; console sequence `preparing completion handoff` then `starting resend loop (interval=1000ms, max=10000ms)` then `attempt 1..N dispatched` then `navigating to redirect URL`.

#### 2. Non-relay merchant sends nothing and redirects in 2 seconds

> ✅ **PASSED** · TerraceFinance (`OL90202-0001`), sandbox and stg

A merchant without the relay shows the handoff, waits two seconds with no message, then redirects. Confirmed with the exact 2001 to 2002 ms delay and zero relay attempts, on both sandbox and stg.

**Evidence:** merchantCode `OL90202-0001`; console `navigating in 2000ms (no postMessage)` then `navigating to redirect URL` at +2001ms (sandbox) and +2002ms (stg); iframe `secure-stg.uownleasing.com/...` for the stg run.

#### 3. Parent receives the burst and advances once

> ✅ **PASSED** · PayTomorrow and TireAgent

The parent page received `uown_success` several times (two per attempt, since both the top and the parent window are posted) and advanced its checkout a single time. This is the idempotency the partner is expected to apply, and it held.

**Evidence:** parent receipt counter reached 6 to 10 for 3 to 5 attempts; a single forward navigation per run.

#### 4. Early iframe teardown stops posting but still redirects

> ✅ **PASSED** · all partner runs

In the real embedded flow the partner advances on receipt and removes the iframe quickly. When that happened, the relay stopped and the child still performed its redirect. Observed naturally in every partner run, with the attempt count dropping as low as 1.

**Evidence:** attempt counts observed 5, 3, 2, 1 across runs; `navigating to redirect URL` present in all.

#### 5. No legacy behavior; app-complete sends no relay

> ✅ **PASSED** · all runs

None of the previous log lines appear, and the app-complete page performs no additional relay.

**Evidence:** absent in all runs: `REDIRECTING...`, `Post Message sent on ... page.`, `Success, Message Sent!`, `... was sent to top window ...`, `... was sent to parent window from appComplete page`.

#### 6. Session-recording flush during handoff

> ✅ **PASSED** · all runs

The recording flush scheduled during the handoff completed without the failure line on a healthy session.

**Evidence:** `Rep flush failed integration reference is null` absent across all 6 runs.

#### 7. Cancel signing without completing

> ⚠️ **Code-confirmed** · runtime not exercised

The code short-circuits a `/signingFailure` redirect immediately (no relay, no 2s wait). Confirmed in source; not exercised at runtime in this cycle.

**Evidence:** `utils/helper.tsx` `scheduleEsignRedirect` (isSigningFailureRedirectUrl branch).

#### 8. Complete-e-sign page path

> ⚠️ **Code-confirmed, entry mapped** · see [OBS-3](#obs-3)

The dedicated complete-e-sign page uses the same handoff with its own source tag. The page renders (phone verification and payment-program steps observed live), but reaching its handoff at runtime requires the bank-verification journey that produces the "finalize verified purchase" link.

**Evidence:** `pages/completeEsign/index.tsx` wires the shared hook with source `completeEsign`; entry built by `LeadService.sendFinalizeEmailAfterVerification`.

## Observations (do not block release)

### OBS-1: The relay almost never reaches 10 attempts in the real flow

> The partner advances and removes the iframe within ~0.5 to 2.5s, so the loop typically fires 1 to 6 attempts, not 10.

<details>
<summary><b>Detail and impact</b></summary>

**When it triggers:** every real embedded ceremony. The partner reacts to the first `uown_success`, advances its checkout, and tears down the iframe, which stops the loop early (the intended early-teardown behavior).

**Impact:** none negative. The 10s / 10-attempt ceiling is a safety net; in practice one message is enough. Worth noting for expectations: the card title "Retry PostMessage Thrice" is nominal, the implemented ceiling is 10 attempts over 10s.

**Evidence:** attempt counts 1, 2, 3, 5, 6 across the partner runs; handoff-to-navigate 0.5 to 2.5s.

</details>

**Classification:** [OBSERVATION]. Expectation-setting.

### OBS-2: Brief lists the console order reversed

> The QA brief shows "resend loop scheduled" before "attempt 1"; the code and runtime log "attempt 1" first.

**Classification:** [CONFIRMED]. Documentation nit, no product impact.

### OBS-3: Partner staging portals are wired to the sandbox environment

> PayTomorrow and PayPair staging portals create applications against UOWN sandbox, so partner end-to-end tests cannot validate stg by switching environment.

<details>
<summary><b>Detail and impact</b></summary>

**When it triggers:** running the partner-portal flows with a non-sandbox environment. The embedded iframe stays `secure-sandbox.uownleasing.com` regardless.

**Impact:** stg was validated through the deploy check (bundle build 2026-07-01T20:47:55Z, identical to sandbox) plus the standalone non-relay run on `secure-stg`. Relay runtime on stg would need a partner portal pointing to stg (Kornerstone has a stg-specific URL).

</details>

**Classification:** [OBSERVATION]. Testing infrastructure.
