### Notes

1. **Sentry replayId is scoped to the tab session.** In CT-01 and CT-02, lead1 and lead2 from the same tab share the same UUID — this is expected Sentry SDK behavior. The AC "recording link present" is validated by the existence of the row in the table, not by UUID uniqueness.
2. **`replayId` is sent on mount of `/{shortCode}/complete`** (`useEffect`), not on final submission. Validated: the row appears within ~15s without completing the application.
